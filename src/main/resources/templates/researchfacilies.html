Core Research Facilities


MSM has made tremendous progress in establishing a robust research infrastructure. The CVRI is over 7000 sq. ft. of research space in the Research Wing (basic science laboratories), Multi-disciplinary Research Center (space adjacent to the Clinical Research Center) and the National Center for Primary Care building (population science). MSM has enjoyed success in obtaining major shared instrument grants from both the NIH and NSF, and has consolidated these resources within institutional Core Facilities that are accessible to all MSM researchers on a fee-for-service basis.
